About Me

Hongyi Du is an undergraduate researcher in the Blender Lab at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. His work focuses on multi-agent large language models, LLM tool learning, and scalable mathematical modeling. He co-develops open benchmarks such as MultiAgentBench and ModelingBench, building reusable agent frameworks that integrate data, simulation, and evaluation at scale.
